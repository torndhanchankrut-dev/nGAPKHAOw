Tips & Etiquette

Curiosity Counter

  • Repeat the menu and say "หนึ่งที่" (one plate)
  • Adjust spice level: "ไม่เผ็ด" (not spicy) to "เผ็ดมาก" (very spicy)
  • Use table condiments to customize your dish
  • Long queues = delicious food worth waiting for
  • Cash is king at most street stalls
